Funerals and Memorials

I would consider it a privilege to help your family say farewell to your loved one, as they would have wished. This is obviously a difficult time for you and I would like to give you all the help and support that you need, at your own pace.
I am Staffordshire-based and am familiar with the crematoriums at Fradley, Sutton Coldfield and Stafford.
However, services can be whereever you wish.

Why choose me...

I am knowledgeable in all aspects of planning for and delivering funeral services and memorials. My training with the Institute of Professional Celebrants included guidance on conducting the most sensitive and difficult funerals, such as for small children, suicides, and estranged family members, as well as for the more straightforward.
