Hello, I'm Lesley and I would love to be your celebrant...

When you're choosing a celebrant, it's so important that you pick the right one for you. Do you like their style? The way they write? The sort of ceremonies they create? Do they seem like someone you could talk to?

Through this website and my social media, I introduce myself and the way that I work. Please do get in touch, if you would like to discuss your requirements for your wedding, funeral, baby naming or other ceremony, and think I might be the right celebrant for you and your loved ones.

I am based between Tamworth and Lichfield, and cover the surrounding areas in Staffordshire, Derbyshire, Warwickshire and Leicestershire.
