A little about me...

In addition to my professional training as a celebrant, I have extensive experience of public speaking and stage work, I am a qualified and experienced project manager and an award-winning events manager, and I am passionate about creating unique ceremonies of love, remembrance and celebration.


I'm married, with two grown-up daughters and six rapidly growing-up grandchildren! I'm very fortunate that we all live near one another, so I see a lot of my grandchildren and family life is very important to me. Between us all, we have eight additional family members, too: two cats, two dogs, three fish and a hamster.


After a long and varied career in often highly pressured roles, I decided to take a step back and focus my energy on occupations where I would be able to channel my skills and creativity into supporting people with their life challenges and celebrations, and this led me to celebrancy. I am also a part-time community support worker and an occasional employability and life skills tutor, working with learning impaired adults.


I completed my celebrancy course in July 2019 and have had the privilege since then to work with so many wonderful people and help them to plan and carry out their ceremonies; ceremonies of loss and of celebration.


I consider it a huge privilege to be a celebrant. My job is to make sure that the family, couple or individual are confident that the ceremony I create with them and for them is unique, memorable and a true reflection of their loving relationships, be it in mourning loss or in celebrating a new life, a transition or a new union.


Whatever the circumstances, my clients can be assured that I have planned their ceremony in detail, working with the venue and everyone else involved to ensure it runs smoothly, so that they can relax and simply focus on their day.


Professional Training and Membership of Professional Body

I am a member of The Institute of Professional Celebrants, with whom I completed a residential training course in July 2019. This course focused on all aspects of celebrancy, including weddings, funerals and naming ceremonies.

Through my membership of the professional body, I am insured for all aspects of delivering my celebrancy work.
